About Us — Haritha Farms
Organic. Ethical. Hormone Free Livestock Farming.
At Haritha Farms, our journey began with a simple belief: when farming follows nature’s rhythm, the food it creates becomes purer, healthier, and far more meaningful. We envisioned a place where hormones, artificial boosters, and shortcuts would never influence how animals grow — a place committed to honest, ethical, and life friendly farming.
That vision found its perfect home in the peaceful landscapes of Kaluva, Jogulamba Gadwal District, Telangana, where clean water, fresh air, and open land allow us to raise livestock the way nature always intended.
Today, Haritha Farms is known for producing hormone free murrel fish, free range country chicken (Natu Kodi), and pasture raised sheep, all nurtured in a natural environment with complete transparency and care.

🌱 Farming the Way Nature Intended
We don’t push animals to grow — we let them flourish naturally.
🐟 Murrel Fish (Snakehead / Jungle Fish)
Our murrel fish are raised in clean, naturally balanced ponds that mirror wild ecosystems.
No hormones. No artificial growth promoters. No forced feeding.
Just pure, naturally grown murrel fish with the firm texture, clean taste, and high nutrition that health conscious families prefer.
🐓 Country Chicken (Desi Natu Kodi)
Our desi birds roam freely, forage naturally, and grow at their own pace.
They’re raised 100% hormone free, resulting in rich flavor, authentic texture, and superior nutrition compared to commercial poultry.
🐑 Sheep (Pasture Raised & Natural)
Our sheep graze peacefully on open organic pastures.
Their growth is natural, their environment stress free, and their diet clean — producing ethically raised, nutrient rich meat.

🌾 Organic Fodder Grown on Our Own Land
Healthy animals start with clean, natural nutrition.
That’s why we grow all our fodder organically on our own farmland in Kaluva, free from:
•	Synthetic fertilizers
•	Pesticides & herbicides
•	Treated or GM seed
Our fields produce nutrient rich grasses, legumes, leafy greens, and natural farm grown ingredients. This gives us complete control over livestock nutrition and ensures full traceability from soil to table — a key reason families searching for hormone free murrel fish, organic country chicken, or natural sheep farming trust Haritha Farms.
Clean fodder → Clean growth → Clean food.
It’s that simple.
